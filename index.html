An accomplished academician and researcher currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Adarsha Mahavidyalaya (PDUAM), Amjonga,Goalpara, Assam. About Me

A dedicated educator, who is deeply involved in teaching, research, and mentoring students, encouraging them to engage in innovative projects and research and published several research papers in reputed journals and conferences, contributing to advancements in AI and related domains. My work reflects a strong commitment to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications in technology.Beyond academics, I am also passionate about promoting digital literacy and technological awareness and also recently start an entrepreneurship journey in Assam.
